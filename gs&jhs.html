Admission Details

Grades School and Junior High School

As a general policy, a student applicant who complies with the admission documents and eligibility requirements may enroll.

Admission Requirements

Incoming KINDER
  • Must be 5 years old by October 31 of the current school year
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
Incoming Grade 7
  • Report Card (SF 9)
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Recent 2 pcs 2x2 ID Picture
  • Latest Income Tax Return or Certificate of Tax Exempt or Certification of Unemployment of Parent/Legal Guardian
Transferees
  • Report Card (SF 9)
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • ESC Certificate (for JHS ESC recipients)

Enrollment Procedures

STEP 1. Accomplish the Online UC Admission Form.

STEP 2. A confirmation message will be sent via SMS or email.

STEP 3. Pay fees through the following payment options . Send proof of payments indicating the student's name to gsjhs.payments@uc-bcf.edu.ph .

Option 1: Cashier Office, UC Main Campus (Monday to Saturday, 8:00AM to 5:00PM)

Option 2: Banks (BDO/ METROBANK/ PNB/ RCBC/ UNIONBANK) no account numbers needed

  • Online Banking thru Bank's Mobile App or Bank's Website, use “BILLS PAYMENT” option
  • Over the Counter using PAYMENT SLIP or BILLS COLLECTION SLIP
Biller/Company Name: University of the Cordilleras
Subscriber's Name: 'Student's Full Name'
Subscriber's Number: 'Student's TEMPORARY ID Number'

STEP 4. The official registration form, student ID and Official UC e-mail account shall be sent via email.
